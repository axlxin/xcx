Product description:

This mini USB LED light can be used for camping and desktop lighting and so on.

The power is less than 0.2W and low heat.

It can be used last more than 100 hours when using a 5000mah power bank.

It is very portable, there is a hole on the board, you can tie it on your key ring.

It is compatible with all standard USB plug device, such as power bank, car/mobile charger, desktop computer, laptop, etc.
