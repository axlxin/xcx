<div>
	<strong>Overview:</strong></div>
<ul>
	<li>
		The SMART Dust sensor can detect the small particles like cigarette smoke and it can distinguish small particles like smoke from large house dust by pulse pattern of singal output.</li>
</ul>
<div>
	<strong>Features:</strong></div>
<ul>
	<li>
		Compact size,light weight(about W59xH46xD18mm,35g)</li>
	<li>
		PWM(pulse width modulation)output(Low pulse output)</li>
	<li>
		Enable to distinguish small particle of cigarette smoke from large house dust.</li>
	<li>
		Constant forced air convection flow by heater risister in dust sensor</li>
	<li>
		Lead free and ROHS directive compliant.</li>
	<li>
		Minimum particle size can be detected over 1 um</li>
	<li>
		(House dust size:avg 20um, yellow dust size:avg 20um, cigartte dust size:avg 1um)</li>
</ul>
<p>
	<strong>Specification:</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Supply voltage:-0.3V to +7V</li>
	<li>
		Operating temperture:-10 °„C to 60 °„C</li>
	<li>
		Storage temperture:-30 °„C to 80 °„C</li>
	<li>
		Current consumption:&lt;100°¿5% mA</li>
	<li>
		Humidity:&lt;85% RH</li>
	<li>
		Singal output:H1&gt;4.5V,Lo&lt;0.7V</li>
</ul>
