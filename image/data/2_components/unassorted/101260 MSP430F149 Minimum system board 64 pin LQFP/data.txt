<div>
	<strong>Overview:</strong></div>
<ul>
	<li>
		MSP430F149 is 16-bit ultra-low power consumption micro controller,with 60KB flash memory,2KB RAM,12 bit ADC,2 USART and a hardware multiplier.</li>
</ul>
<div>
	<strong>Features:</strong></div>
<ul>
	<li>
		USB interface,support USB powered,USB download,marquee.</li>
	<li>
		You can do serial communication experiment through USB interface and PC.</li>
	<li>
		8 flash LED</li>
	<li>
		Standard pin pitch:2.54mm</li>
	<li>
		NRF905 wireless module interface.</li>
	<li>
		Copper cylinder on the board,improving the durability and reliability of the circuit board.</li>
	<li>
		Minimum system</li>
</ul>
