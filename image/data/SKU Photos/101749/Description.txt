<p>
	<strong>Functional features:</strong></p>
<p>
	1. Eight digits IV-18 VFD tubes display. (1980-1990 made in USSR).<br />
	2. Display hour, minute and second (12 or 24 hours format optional).<br />
	3. Display year, month and day and Chinese lunar date (YY-MM-DD or MM-DD-YY.DD-MM-YY.YY-DD-MM format optional).<br />
	4. Leader zero ON/OFF function.<br />
	5. GPS adjust, automatic adjust date and time in real time. GPS status display (GPS is offered as an option).<br />
	6. GMT time zone functions, the clock will calculate the local time base on satellite.<br />
	7. Temperature display(? or ? temperature unit optional).<br />
	8. Two alarm clock(set on or off different alarm respectively).<br />
	9. Auto date and temperature circulating display. Also can set interval for circulation.<br />
	10. Timer function which can supply longest 23 hours 59 minutes and 59 seconds.<br />
	11. Power on/off timer, also can set the on/off time respectively. You can also check time and date easily after auto power off.<br />
	12. 8 levels brightness adjustable, or auto brightness in different environment.<br />
	13. Button sound on/off.<br />
	14. IR remote controller operation supplied.<br />
	<br />
	<strong>Please check <a href="http://vonnieda.org/tc18">here</a> for more details.</strong></p>
